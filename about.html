About Us

Our company was founded in 2022 by Kali Connor. She was always passionate about climate change and its effects on the Ocean, but in 2020 when she took an Oceanogrpahy class, a spark ignited in her heart. She knew she needed to do something, so she started researching about companies who clean the ocean and how they find the funds to do so.

And so, Ocean Motion was born!

We create and manufacture bracelets made out of 100% recylced material from the Ocean. By selling these environmentally friendly accessories, we raise enough money to get out on the beautiful ocean and sift trash that collects there. We collect all trash and pollutants, whether it is on the surface of the water or wrapped around the coral reefs below. No trash is too big for us!
